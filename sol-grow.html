What is GROW with SAP?

Launched in March 2023, GROW with SAP is designed for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) aiming to move from traditional ERP systems to modern cloud-based solutions. It offers an end-to-end package that includes cloud ERP software, adoption services, community support, and learning resources to ensure a smooth and effective implementation.

The Technical Framework

GROW with SAP brings together the public edition of SAP S/4HANA Cloud with adoption and acceleration services, preconfigured best practices, embedded AI and automation capabilities, and the SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP). With Accelerate for GROW with SAP, organizations can fast-track their cloud ERP deployment using a structured framework.

Strategic Cloud Model

GROW with SAP is a strategic offering for organizations seeking a cloud ERP solution that can be deployed rapidly, securely, and cost-efficiently. It is exclusively available as a public cloud solution within this operating model, offering clear operational predictability and immediate scalability.

Public Cloud Environment

In a public cloud environment, multiple customers share the same infrastructure and software, enabling easy scalability. The system is fully hosted and managed by SAP, reducing the need for extensive internal IT maintenance and allowing corporate teams to focus on standard growth objectives.

Ready-to-Run Advantages

GROW with SAP leverages preconfigured best practices that can be adopted right away, making it easier to establish a fully functional ERP system in the public cloud. SAP describes this offering as a "ready-to-run cloud ERP solution" that cuts down on deployment friction and legacy system constraints.

Standardization & Fit

It delivers a cloud ERP system that is ready to use, with a predictable and accelerated implementation process. It is ideal for mid-sized organizations, as well as small and medium-sized subsidiaries of larger enterprises, that are planning a transition to the cloud while prioritizing standardization.

Preconfigured Solutions

Provides ready-made business processes built on industry best practices, enabling organizations to deploy the system quickly—typically within a modern, structured four to eight weeks timeline while keeping core operational structures clean.

Artificial Intelligence Integration

The solution includes built-in embedded AI capabilities and smart data analytical insights that support better decision-making processes, streamline complex enterprise workflows, and intelligently support automation goals.

Low-Code Environment

Using next-gen development tools such as SAP Build, business users can adapt, extend, and safely scale workflows with minimal coding or structural interference, making it easier to modify properties as specific market requirements shift over time.
